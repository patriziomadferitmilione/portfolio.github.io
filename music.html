It was my dream, I wanted to be a Rock 'n' Roll Star.

My band was called Bitter Almonds,

We stayed together for only about 1 year, and recorded 3 singles in the studio.

Then I had a solo phase, and recorded 3 more singles, in my room.

Before I recorded those singles, I recorded a bunch of demos throughout the years, some of them made it in this album.
